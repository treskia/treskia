Connect Gmail, read-only

Sign in with Google. Treskia can read email — it can never send, delete, or change anything. Revoke access anytime from your Google account.

Your phone does the reading

A filter finds bank alerts, bank-specific patterns extract the details, and an on-device AI model handles the rest. Your emails are processed by your iPhone's chip — not our servers. We don't have servers that see them.

See where money goes

Every card in one place: merchants, categories, trends, search. Built for Indian banks — HDFC, ICICI, Kotak, SBI, Axis and more.

Private by architecture, not by promise

Treskia's access to Gmail is limited to reading transaction-alert emails to show you your own spending. All parsing and AI inference run on your device. We never transfer Gmail data to our servers, never sell it, and never use it for ads.

Treskia's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
